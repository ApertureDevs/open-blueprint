Open Blueprint

An Open Plaform to increase creativity

Open Blueprint is an open platform to help makers to create, design, fix and improve projects.

Make

Open Blueprint provide an repository of Blueprints. Blueprints are makers projects shared with the community. Each Blueprints explain every step to make what you whant with the community help.

Design

Open Blueprint can help you to bring to life your project with the community contribution.

Contribute

Open mean contribution and everybody can contribute to a Blueprint or even to Open Blueprint platform, no mather what you skill are.

Every contribution are welcome, it can be :

  • an enhancement proposal
  • a problem report
  • a simple question
